# EGU26 Short Course Session Materials

This page gathers the online material for the EGU26 short course on machine-learning-based climate downscaling.

## Course files

- [Dataset files to provide](./DATASETS_TO_PROVIDE.md)
- The presentation PDF and sample notebook will be added to this folder before publication.
- Methodology and environment notes will be published alongside the final session package.

## What attendees will find here

The material is organized to support both a guided short-course session and later self-study:

- the presentation explains the scientific context and workflow
- the notebook gives a practical runnable example
- the dataset list identifies the files needed to reproduce the demonstrated steps
- the methodology and environment notes give higher-level guidance for participants who want to go further

## Notes for attendees

- The notebook is intended as a guided example, not as a full production workflow.
- Some demonstrations may use reduced temporal or spatial windows for portability.
- A trained checkpoint can be reused only when the data and preprocessing setup remain compatible with the training configuration.
